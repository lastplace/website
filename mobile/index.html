The Story

With a shoestring budget and a compulsive need to make stuff, we're turning Last Place into the funnest place to skate and shop for everything you destroy in the process.

With the help of a team of designers, nerds and tinkerers, we pooled our collective resources to build up some initial inventory and remodel the storefront. We're also putting our design studio chops to good use, screen printing high-quality American Apparel Tees, hoodies and decks featuring our own graphics.

Come by and skate with us, we appreciate your support!
